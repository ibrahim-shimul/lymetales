Tracking & Important Details

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to monitor your parcel's progress locally and internationally. You can also track your order directly on our site using your order number.

Track My Order

Key Considerations

  • Production Lead Times: Production occurs Monday–Friday. Orders placed on weekends will enter the print queue the following Monday.
  • Holiday Shipping: During high-demand seasons (Christmas, Back-to-School), we recommend adding 2–3 days to standard estimates.
  • Custom Nature: As books are personalized, we cannot reroute packages once they have left our facility. Please ensure your address is 100% correct.
