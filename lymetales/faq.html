Because every book is entirely custom-made, we send files to the printers almost instantly. You have a 2-hour window after purchase to contact our support team if you notice a spelling mistake or need an address change.

If the error was made during your configuration and the 2-hour window has passed, the book will print as submitted. However, please reach out to us! We often offer a steep discount for re-prints if an honest mistake occurred.

Since your book is uniquely printed upon order:

  • Standard Shipping: 5–8 business days
  • Express Delivery: 3–5 business days

You will receive a tracking link via email the moment the book is shipped.

Because personalized books cannot be resold, we cannot accept general returns. However, if your book arrives damaged or has a printing defect, we will replace it immediately at zero cost to you.
